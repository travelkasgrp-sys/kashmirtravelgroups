Everything you need to know before booking your Kashmir tour package.

The best time to visit Kashmir depends on what you're looking for. Spring (April–June) is perfect for the famous tulip gardens and Shikara rides on Dal Lake. Summer (July–August) is ideal for trekking in Sonamarg and Pahalgam. Autumn (September–October) offers breathtaking fall colours. Winter (December–February) is the best time for skiing at Gulmarg. We operate year-round and can help you plan the perfect trip for any season.

Our Kashmir tour packages start from ₹18,999 per person for a 5N/6D family package, ₹22,499 per person for a 6N/7D adventure package, and ₹28,999 per person for a 7N/8D honeymoon package. All packages include accommodation, transfers, and guided sightseeing. We also create fully custom itineraries to suit your group size and budget — request a free quote to get started.
